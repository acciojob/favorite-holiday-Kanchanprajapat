Diwali


Diwali, the festival of lights, joy, prosperity and happiness will be celebrated this year on October 24, 2022. It is a five day long celebration that commences with Dhanteras and ends with Bhaiya Dooj. The festival of Diwali is celebrated on the darkest night of the year in the Hindu month of Kartik. This festival is being celebrated on Kartik Amavasya.
